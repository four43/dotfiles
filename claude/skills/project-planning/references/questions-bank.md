# Extended Question Bank by Project Type

Use these to supplement the core discovery questions in SKILL.md when you know
the project type. Pick the most relevant — don't ask all of them.

---

## Software / Product Development

- What platforms or devices need to be supported?
- Are there existing systems this must integrate with?
- Who manages authentication / user accounts?
- What's the expected user volume (concurrent, daily, monthly)?
- Are there data privacy or compliance requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2)?
- What's the deployment environment (cloud provider, on-prem, hybrid)?
- Is there an API contract with external teams?
- What's the rollback / rollout strategy?
- Will there be a beta / pilot phase before full launch?
- How will the system be monitored post-launch?

---

## Marketing / Campaign

- What is the primary call to action?
- What channels will be used (email, social, paid, SEO, events)?
- Who is the target audience / persona?
- What's the campaign budget?
- How will leads or conversions be tracked?
- Are there brand guidelines or legal review requirements?
- What's the approval chain for creative assets?
- Are there localization or translation needs?

---

## Operations / Process Improvement

- What is the current process and where does it break down?
- Who performs this process today, and how many people are affected?
- What tools are currently used?
- What would a 10x better version of this process look like?
- Are there compliance or audit requirements?
- How will success be measured (time saved, error rate, cost)?
- What's the change management plan (training, documentation)?

---

## Research / Analysis

- What specific question(s) needs to be answered?
- What decisions will this research inform?
- What data sources are available?
- What's the acceptable level of confidence / statistical significance?
- Are there known biases or limitations to account for?
- Who is the audience for the findings?
- What format should the output take (report, presentation, dataset)?

---

## Event / Launch

- What type of event (internal, external, virtual, in-person, hybrid)?
- Expected attendance?
- What are the must-have vs. nice-to-have elements?
- What vendors or partners are involved?
- What's the contingency plan if something goes wrong day-of?
- How will attendee experience / satisfaction be measured?

---

## General (use for any project type)

- What does "good" look like vs. "great"?
- What would make stakeholders feel this project failed?
- Is there a comparable project or benchmark to reference?
- What's the communication cadence during the project?
- Who needs to be informed vs. who needs to approve?
- What are the top 3 things that could cause this to slip?
