## Summary: Slide Design Patterns

### Layout

- **Cover slides:** full-bleed cinematic photo, white text overlay, logo in corner
- **Content slides:** either full dark background OR white/light background — not mixed
- **Split layouts:** dark text panel + full-bleed image side by side
- Logo always anchored to a corner (typically bottom-left)
- Date and slide number bottom-right, "Internal" label bottom-center when applicable

### Typography on Slides

- Overlines: small uppercase "THIS IS VAISALA XWEATHER" (approved uppercase exception)
- Headlines: large, Light weight
- Body: Regular weight, often in 2-column layout
- Stats: large Light-weight numbers with small Regular-weight labels beneath

### Charts & Data Visualization

- **Color order for chart series:** Misty Green Extra Light, Xweather Black, Misty Gray (and their shades)
- All chart elements are **flat** — no shadows, no 3D, no gradients on bars/slices
- Bars and blocks have **squared-off corners**
- Gridlines are subtle light gray
- Large stat callouts use Light or Medium weight

### Dark vs Light Slides

- **Dark slides** (Xweather Black bg): used for hero/cover, stats, maps, concept diagrams — often with subtle atmospheric photo underneath
- **Light slides** (white/gray-20 bg): used for charts, data, detailed content
